Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cucumber (diced)
  • 1 cup tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (grated)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp mint leaves (optional, finely chopped)
  • Feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Did You Know?

Greek salad, or Horiatiki, is traditionally a "village salad" in Greece. It features fresh ingredients like tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta, served without lettuce for a truly authentic taste!

Steps to Make Greek Salad

  1. Wash and dice the cucumber and tomatoes.
  2. Grate the carrots, and finely chop the coriander and mint leaves.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, carrots, and herbs.
  4. Crumble or dice the feta cheese and gently toss into the salad.
  5. Mix 1 tbsp lemon juice with a pinch of salt to prepare the dressing.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss lightly.
  7. Serve fresh and enjoy!
