Dish of the Day: Greek Salad
Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cucumber (diced)
  • 1 cup tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (grated)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp mint leaves (optional, finely chopped)
  • Feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Make Greek Salad

  1. Wash and dice the cucumber and tomatoes.
  2. Grate the carrots, and finely chop the coriander and mint leaves.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, add the diced cucumber, tomatoes, grated carrots, and herbs.
  4. Crumble or dice the feta cheese and gently toss it into the salad.
  5. In a small bowl, mix 1 tbsp of lemon juice with a pinch of salt. Adjust to taste.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss everything together lightly to combine.
  7. Serve immediately as a fresh and vibrant dish!
