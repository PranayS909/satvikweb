Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cucumber (diced)

  • 1 cup tomatoes (diced)

  • 1/2 cup carrots (grated)

  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)

  • 1 tbsp mint leaves (optional, finely chopped)

  • feta chese

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • Salt to taste

Steps to Make Greek Salad

Prepare the Ingredients: Wash and dice the cucumber and tomatoes. Grate the carrots, and finely chop the coriander and mint leaves. Combine the Vegetables: In a large mixing bowl, add the diced cucumber, tomatoes, grated carrots, and herbs (coriander and mint). Add Feta Cheese: Crumble or dice the feta cheese and gently toss it into the salad. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon of lemon juice with a pinch of salt. Adjust the quantity of lemon juice or salt to suit your taste. Mix and Serve: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss everything together lightly to combine.
