Did You Know?

The unripe jackfruit has a fibrous, meaty texture that resembles shredded chicken, making it a favorite plant-based alternative in vegetarian and vegan dishes. It absorbs spices beautifully, enhancing the curry's flavor profile.

Steps to Make Jackfruit Curry

  1. Cut the jackfruit into medium-sized pieces and soak in water with turmeric for 15-20 minutes to reduce sap.
  2. Soak Bengal gram for at least an hour and grind into a smooth paste.
  3. Deep fry small portions of the Bengal gram paste into pakoras and set aside.
  4. Boil jackfruit in a pressure cooker for 1 whistle, then sauté with potatoes in oil.
  5. Prepare a spice mix using turmeric, coriander, cumin, and chili powder. Sauté with ginger, bay leaves, and dry red chilies.
  6. Combine the spice mix with sautéed jackfruit and potatoes, add water for gravy, and bring to a boil. Add garam masala.
  7. Add fried pakoras, cook for 2-3 minutes, and serve hot.
